# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what's best for the project and its users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior in issues/PRs/discussions.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response
deemed appropriate to the circumstances.

## Attribution
